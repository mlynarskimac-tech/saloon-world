This S 580 Lang represents the most complete expression of the current S-Class generation. Ordered new from Mercedes-Benz with a comprehensive specification that prioritises discretion and comfort in equal measure. The Obsidian Black Metallic finish is one of the most sophisticated colour choices available — deep, complex and commanding without ostentation. Combined with the AMG Line bodywork and 21" AMG wheels, the presence is unmistakable.

The interior is appointed in Nappa Exclusive leather in Buche Brown — a warm, rare combination that contrasts beautifully with the piano black trim. The Executive Rear package transforms the rear compartment into a first-class environment with reclining seats, massage, and climate control. A Burmester High-End 4D surround system completes the sensory experience.
