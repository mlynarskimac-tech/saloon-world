Our criteria

What we look
for in a vehicle

01

Exceptional specification

Factory options, Manufaktur finishes, bespoke interiors. The more considered the build, the better the story we can tell.

02

Verifiable history

Full service records, single ownership or well-documented provenance. Transparency is non-negotiable for buyers in this segment.

03

Condition befitting the price

We present cars honestly. The condition must match the asking price — and ideally, exceed expectations.

04

2020 model year or newer

We focus on the current generation of ultra-premium saloons. Exceptions made for truly exceptional earlier cars.
